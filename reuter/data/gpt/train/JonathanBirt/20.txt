Allied Colloids, one of the world's leading specialty chemical companies, has announced that it will continue to pursue organic growth strategies despite the recent $390 million acquisition of CPS Chemical Co Inc.
The acquisition of CPS Chemical Co Inc., a leading producer of specialty chemicals and materials, is expected to significantly expand Allied Colloids' product portfolio and increase its global presence. However, the company has made it clear that it does not intend to rely solely on acquisitions to fuel its growth.

"While we are excited about the opportunities that the CPS Chemical acquisition presents, we remain committed to growing our business through innovation, research and development, and by building strong relationships with our customers."

By focusing on organic growth, the company is able to maintain these strong relationships, which are vital to its success.
