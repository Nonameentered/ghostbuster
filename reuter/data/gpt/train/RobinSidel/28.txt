Fort Lauderdale billionaire Wayne Huizenga’s empire continues to expand with his recent acquisition of Alamo Rent-A-Car, a deal reported to be worth $625 million. Huizenga, founder of Waste Management, Blockbuster Video, and AutoNation, is shifting his focus towards rental cars as he hopes to maintain and expand his status as a top player in the transportation industry. Alamo Rent-A-Car will join his existing car rental companies, including National and Enterprise. With the acquisition of Alamo, Huizenga’s car rental business is expected to generate more than $3 billion in annual revenue.
