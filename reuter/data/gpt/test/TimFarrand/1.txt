Compass Group CEO, Dominic Blakemore, has predicted significant growth potential in the trend for catering outsourcing. He states that outsourcing catering services can improve the quality and variety of food offered while also providing better value for money. Blakemore also believes that consumers are becoming increasingly discerning and looking for more personalized experiences when it comes to dining options. Compass Group has seen strong revenue growth in recent years, and Blakemore feels that catering outsourcing trend will continue to be a key driver of this growth. He encourages businesses to consider outsourcing catering services to achieve better quality and satisfaction for their customers.
