Leading British gambling company Ladbroke Group Plc has reported that the mainland European hotels sector is gradually recovering from the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic. The company, which owns the Hilton Hotel chain, has seen a positive trend in occupancy rates and bookings in several countries, including France, Spain, and Italy. The group also expects its digital revenues to increase sharply as more customers choose to book rooms online. Ladbroke's CEO, Jason Robbins, has expressed optimism about the company's prospects for the rest of the year and beyond, as the world continues to emerge from the pandemic.
