Citizens Utilities Co has announced plans to become the top telephone company in the US through acquisitions. The company's CEO stated that they are aggressively looking to acquire other telecom companies in order to expand their operations and provide better services to customers. Citizens Utilities Co has already acquired several smaller telephone companies in the past, but their latest move signals a major push towards becoming a major player in the US telecom industry. The company has stated that they will do whatever it takes to achieve their goal of becoming the top telephone company in the country.
