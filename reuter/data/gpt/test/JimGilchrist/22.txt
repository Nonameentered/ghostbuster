Cathay Pacific has announced the expansion of its cargo services with the opening of a new office in Auckland, New Zealand. The move will enable the airline to offer more frequent and efficient air freight options for customers in the region. The new office will allow Cathay Pacific to link businesses and industries in New Zealand with its global cargo network. The addition of the Auckland office highlights the airline's commitment to developing its cargo business and increasing its presence in key markets. It is hoped that this move will also contribute to the growth of the New Zealand economy.
