The aim of the practical was to illustrate and see practical application and importance of various packaging materials.
The effectiveness of different materials and methods was considered using various food materials under different conditions
Packaging plays a vital role in maintaining foods in a healthy or fit state for eventual consumption. Packaging reduces infestation, spoilage, water uptake or loss, contamination and oxidation.
The packaging materials available are as variable and complex as the nature of foods available
Furthermore , on average around 25% of the ex-factory cost of consumer foods is for their packaging
Packaging comes into play in the processing, preservation, marketing and distribution of many foods
