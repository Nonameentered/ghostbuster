The growth of a plant is influenced by various endogenous and exogenous factors. The components of the environment in which the plant lives, such as temperature, the duration and the intensity of available sunlight, alter the favorableness of the growth. With time, the plant progress the developmental stages which starts from the vegetative growth of the plant body into the reproductive growth.
The aim of this experiment was to analyse the processes of the development of a plant, the Dwarf French Bean (Phaseolus vulgaris).
