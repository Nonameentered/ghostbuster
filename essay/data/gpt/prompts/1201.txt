What is the future of the Internet Age, and what opportunities does it offer for e-business and e-commerce? Discuss the development history of the Internet, the opportunities that have arisen in e-marketplace, and the future drivers of Internet in business circumstances. Consider the benefits of e-enabled business, including the reduction in customer service costs and improvements in time-based customer delivery performance schedules. Additionally, discuss how companies are using the Internet to evaluate business counterparties, competitors, and customer needs, and how the Internet can be used for optimal resource allocation and aggregation. Finally, consider the evolution phase of the Internet, and how the industry value chain may progress into a Value Trust Network, supported by a global knowledge network and enhanced security for collaboration and integration between well-branded organizations.
