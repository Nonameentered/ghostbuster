What is the legal status of non-state actors involved in asymmetrical warfare, specifically in regards to the limitations on the means and methods of warfare set by the law of war and the protection of International Humanitarian Law rules? Explore the controversial and ambiguous aspects of the phenomenon of terrorism, the problems raised by the Guantanamo Bay detention center, and the possibilities and difficulties of the codification of the concept of terrorism. Additionally, how might an increase of protection for non-state actors change the outcome of the war, and what are the potential consequences of codifying terrorism on the relationship between International Humanitarian Law and Human Rights law?
