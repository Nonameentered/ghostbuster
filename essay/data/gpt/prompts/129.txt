Why was Calvinism the most successful brand of Protestantism in the second half of the sixteenth century throughout Europe, and what were the factors behind its success? How did the spread of Calvinist writings, the missionary zeal of John Calvin and his followers, the resilient structure of the movement, and the death of Calvin contribute to the success of Calvinism? In examining the impact of Calvinism in France as a case study, how did the movement spread throughout Europe and what was its appeal particularly among those involved in trade?
