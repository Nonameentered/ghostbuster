Compare and contrast Descartes and Hume's approaches to scepticism. What are the key differences in their methods, and to what extent can their conclusions be applied to different projects? Consider the distinction between 'doubt,' 'suspension of judgement,' and 'false beliefs,' as well as the importance of the requirement of indubitability in knowledge. Analyze Hume's argument for the existence of the external world, and examine Descartes' method of casting doubt on both reason and perception. What are the strengths and weaknesses of each approach, and to what extent do they rely on assumptions about human nature?
