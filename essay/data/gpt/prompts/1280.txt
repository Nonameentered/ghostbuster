What is the anti-competitive effect of conglomerate mergers under EC law? Discuss the characterization of conglomerate mergers, the criteria used to evaluate them, and the main concerns of European authorities such as the European Commission and the European Court of Justice. Evaluate the different approaches taken before and after the Tetra Laval/Sidel case and analyze the standards of proof required to establish the potential harm of these mergers, particularly in relation to the portfolio effect and leveraging. Discuss the role of courts in checking the conduct and decisions of the Commission and evaluate the criticisms raised by the Commission regarding the CFI settlement.
