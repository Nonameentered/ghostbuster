What are the causes and resolutions of recent banking crises, and how can they be addressed to prevent future collapses? Use the Argentine banking crisis as a case study to analyze the vulnerabilities and factors that trigger banking collapses, policies to be implemented when a crisis arises, and recommendations for improving the situation of the banking system and avoiding vulnerabilities in the future. Also, explore the hypothesis of financial hysteresis and its impact on credit to the private sector in developing countries.
