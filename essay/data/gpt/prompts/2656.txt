What ethical values did the Ancient Egyptians hold regarding the use of magic, and how do these values compare to the magical techniques employed in State Magic, Funerary Magic, and Popular Magic? In particular, the essay examines the notion of Ma'at and Heka as key concepts in magic, and analyzes specific rituals and spells that may contradict ethical criteria. The essay also explores the use of Greek Magical Papyri and the changing nature of magic in Egyptian history.
