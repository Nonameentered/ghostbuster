To what extent was there a Tory Party in existence before the Great Reform Act? Analyze the organizational changes in British politics in the decades leading up to the Act, including the development of political parties, the role of Pitt the Younger in hindering the creation of a Tory Party, and the development of a cohesive ideology and policy platform within the Tory party. Evaluate the arguments for and against the existence of a Tory Party during this time period by examining the loyalty of MPs to the government, the development of party institutions, and internal factions within the party.
