What is anomie and how is it linked to social harmony? Explore the concept of anomie and its various forms, and examine how Durkheim's functionalist perspective aimed to apply sociological knowledge to create social harmony and reduce levels of anomie. Consider how anomie is linked to suicide, crime, mental disorders, and addiction and examine how social integration and moral consensus can create a stable society. Discuss Durkheim's concept of mechanical and organic solidarity and how a modern society based on mutual social interdependence can reduce levels of anomie.
