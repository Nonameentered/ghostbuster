How did the views of the Enlightenment affect colonial life in New South Wales from the date of the first settlement in the region until the middle of the nineteenth century? Analyze the effect of the Enlightenment in terms of the reasoning behind transporting convicts to New South Wales, the methods of punishment and rehabilitation the authorities imposed on the convicts once in Australia, the colonists' attitudes towards and treatment of the Aborigines, as well as the agricultural outlook and methodology of the colonists.
