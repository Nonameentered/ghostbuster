How do institutions promote economic growth? Analyze the role of institutions in shaping economic performance through an investigation of historical governmental structures, long-distance trade, and economic change. Consider the ways in which institutions can provide incentives conducive to growth and mitigate the fundamental problem of exchange. Assess the persistence of underdevelopment in third-world countries in contrast to the rise of the "European Miracle" and consider the role of institutional frameworks in the development of distinct economic performances.
