What is the connection between birth order, personality, and parental treatment? Does birth order have a significant effect on a person's extroversion/introversion? This essay explores conflicting findings from previous research and investigates the causal links between birth order and personality, through parental treatment, in 34 eldest siblings, 30 youngest siblings, and 19 middle/only children. The essay presents the experimental hypothesis and discusses the descriptive statistics and ANOVA results.
