What are the gendered social and economic effects of the mail order bride industry? Using the Filipina women - Japanese, American, and Canadian men commodity chain as an example, examine the various elements of the industry, including the roles of the International Marriage Agencies and the States involved. Discuss how the gendered structures of this industry affect individuals at every level of the chain, whether they are acting by choice or under duress, and explore both the bridges of opportunity and vises of exploitation that are created by this global sex chain.
